Real-Time Human-AI Music

Eliminating and masking the two-second latency wall

We examine two 2025 papers that each address the same problem, the approximately two-second latency wall that blocks real-time human-AI musical collaboration, through distinct strategies. Ghost in the Keys eliminates the bottleneck at the inference level via continuous KV-cache prefill on a Yamaha Disklavier. ReaLJam masks it through probabilistic foresight, a commitment protocol, and a waterfall display.
